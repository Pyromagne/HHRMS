Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 22 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how HHRMS ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information in connection with our Hospital Human Resource Management System ("HRMS"). This policy applies to individuals who interact with the HRMS, including employees, job applicants, and other users.

1. Information We Collect:

We may collect the following types of personal information for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

  • Employee Information: Name, contact details, employee ID, job title, department, work history, and other employment-related information.
  • Job Applicant Information: Resume, cover letter, contact details, education, work history, and other information submitted during the application process.
  • Access and Usage Information: Log-in details, IP addresses, device information, and other data generated through the use of the HRMS.

2. How We Use Your Information:

We use personal information for the following purposes:

  • HR Management: Managing employee records, payroll, benefits administration, and other human resource functions.
  • Recruitment: Evaluating job applications, conducting interviews, and making employment decisions.
  • System Maintenance: Monitoring and improving the performance, security, and functionality of the HRMS.

3. Information Sharing:

We may share personal information with third parties for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. These third parties may include:

  • Service Providers: External companies that help us operate the HRMS and provide related services.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal requests.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Changes are effective upon posting, so please check this page periodically for updates.

7. Contact Us:

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at bcp.hhrms@sample.com.

By using our HRMS, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
